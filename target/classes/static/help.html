How to Play

Guess the Word

You have 6 chances to guess a 5-letter word. Type a word using the keyboard and press ENTER to submit.

Color Feedback

After each guess, the tiles change color to show how close you are.

Winning

If all 5 tiles turn green, you win! If you don't guess the word in 6 tries, the word is revealed.
